THE GOSPEL OF JESUS CHRIST

The Gospel of Jesus Christ is the good news that God sent His Son to the world to save sinners. Jesus, being fully God and fully man, lived a sinless life, died on the cross for our sins, and rose again on the third day. Through faith in Him, we receive forgiveness and eternal life.

CHURCH

The church is the body of Christ, a community of believers gathered in His name.

What is Church?

The Church is not merely a building, but the congregation of those who believe in Jesus Christ. It serves as a spiritual family, a place of worship, fellowship, and teaching.

Purpose of the Church from the Holy Bible

According to the Bible, the purpose of the Church is to glorify God by worshiping Him (John 4:24), spreading the gospel (Matthew 28:19-20), equipping believers (Ephesians 4:12), and serving others in love (Galatians 5:13).
